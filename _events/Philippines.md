<h3>PhilGEOS</h3>
<p>Organised by the University of the Philippines Department of Geodetic
Engineering (UP DGE) and Training Center for Applied Geodesy and Photogrammetry
(TCAGP), PhilGEOS is a gathering of geospatial technology developers, researchers,
professionals, users, and students.</p>
<p>For more information, visit <a rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">https://www.facebook.com/philgeosymposium</a>
</p>
